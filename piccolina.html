Piccolina Gelato E Altra Roba

Piccolina Gelato E Altra Roba is a dessert and gelato store front created by the owners of Nodo, Italian Restaurant.

Branding uses an Italian-inspired hand written font and a cone/heart illustration to symbolize 'piccolina', which means 'little.' Purples, teals and terracotta are the colour palette to create a Miami-meets-Italian vibe for this quaint, 'piccolina' space.
