Queenston Mile

Queenston Mile is a new winery located in Niagara-on-the-Lake.

Web design features moody imagery, giving it a warm, rustic feel while showcasing the winery's upscale, yet down-to-earth appeal. Simple navigation helps the user easily peruse the site, while driving users to the 'Shop' page, where most traffic is desired. Warm and inviting lifestyle and product shot photography is used to appeal to the users' senses and draw them to visit the winery or purchase products direct from the site.
