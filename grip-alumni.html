Grip Alumni

The alumni page for the Grip apprenticeship website features all present and past apprentices by year, role and status.

Design features personalized, greyscale imagery that portrays a fun, laidback vibe. Orange badges are used for a pop of colour against the grey backdrop and represent the year each member completed their apprenticeship at Grip. Formatting uses orange hovered overlays to indicate that the apprentice was hired by Grip and still works there. Black overlays indicate that the apprentice is available for hire or no longer works at Grip. Page provides a filtering option at the top for year, role, hired apprentices and apprentices available for hire.
