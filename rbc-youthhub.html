RBC Student Solutions

The RBC Student Solutions site was created to house all resources from the RBC corporate site that are geared towards youth, like specific banking products and rewards programs.

Design features fun, energetic photography, colourful illustrations and bold, large typography to appeal to today's modern, tech-savvy youth. Big tiles and direct calls to action are used to navigate the user through the site experience in an effective way. A separate, tailored site for this demographic, provides a more customized and simple user experience, increasingly the likelihood of product use and engagement.
