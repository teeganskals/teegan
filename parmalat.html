Parmalat

Parmalat produces milk and dairy products, cultured products, cheese products and table spreads with many different brands.

The website lays out all the corporate information of the company, as well as information on all their sub brands in an organized and precise way. Design uses light and calming imagery with a saturated white overlay to keep the overall imagery effect consistent throughout the user experience. Blue accents divide the sections throughout the site to keep them separate and distinct. Iconography illustrates different sections and provides visual appeal to the simplistic design.
